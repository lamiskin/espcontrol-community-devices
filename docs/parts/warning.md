::: danger Unofficial community devices repo
This site covers **unofficial, community-maintained** EspControl builds for
panels the upstream project does not support. Any issue you encounter on these
devices must be logged **only** to the
[community repo issues](https://github.com/lamiskin/espcontrol-community-devices/issues)
— **never** to the upstream EspControl repo. The upstream project holds **no
responsibility** for support or updates of these builds, and the experience on
community hardware may be **buggier** than on officially supported panels.
:::
